Rats And Roombas

Game Trailer

Overview

Rats and Roombas is a 2 Player Local Co-Operative PC Game where the players control two hungry rats with unique abilities. Scavenge for food and collect enough cheese and your abilities to combat the AI Roomba defending the room. Rats and Roombas is played on a Keyboard and Mouse and PS4 Dualshock Controllers respectively.

Role

Responsible for implementing core gameplay systems, including player movement, enemy AI and targeting. Developed mechanics such as teleportation, knockback on collision, and a player life system. Also implemented collectible systems and unique player abilities, contributing to overall game flow and player experience.
