I Want To Go Home...

Game Trailer

Overview

I Want To Go Home... is a Single Player VR Simulation where the player is a Singapore Polytechnic Student who just wants to go home after a long day, but is stopped by their classmate who needs help finding their lost items.

Role

This was an individual projects with the VR simulation template provided by the school. I worked on extra mechanics such as dragging of objects, dialogue system, and timer to make the game feel more immersive.
