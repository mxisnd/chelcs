Overview

Too Cube is a Single Player Cube Puzzle Game where the player gets sucked into an alternate dimension and have their soul split in half. The player has to get through different levels, controlling two cubes at once, navigating through obstacles in each respective worlds to get back to your original dimension and fuse their souls back into one.

Role

Independently designed and implemented all core gameplay systems, including climbing, pushing blocks, pressure plate activation upon block placement, and animations
