Balloon Joyride

Game Trailer

Overview

Balloon Joyride is a Single Player Endless Runner where the main goal of the player is to run and avoid obstacles to gain points, and collect balloons and use them to fly whilst simultaneously avoiding the obstacles in the air.

Role

First exposure to Unreal Engine 4 Blueprints. Designed and implemented all core gameplay systems, including player movement, unique obstacles, points system, collection of coins, magnets and balloons, and flight system.
